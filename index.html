  Attack on Titan (Japanese: 進撃の巨人) is a Japanese dark fantasy anime television series, adapted from the manga series of the same name by Hajime Isayama.

  The series premiered on April 7, 2013, and concluded on November 5, 2023.

  It has aired on MBS TV (first two seasons) and NHK General TV (third season onwards).
